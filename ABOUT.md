# Mission Control — Quick Guide

Mission Control ("Mammoth") is the Disruptors Media command center — one place to run
content, ads, SEO, tasks, and client relationships. You pick a client at the top right,
and every page shows that client's world.

## Signing in
You log in with an email and password. Two kinds of accounts:
- **Admins** (the Disruptors team) see every client and can switch between them.
- **Clients** see only their own workspace.

Admins can add a new client right from the app using **"+ New Client"** in the client menu.

## What each page does

- **Overview** — the dashboard: headline numbers, a performance chart, the live activity
  feed, and the current task queue. Your at-a-glance morning check.
- **Paid Advertising** — ad performance, accounts, AI ad-copy, and channel scaling. If a
  client isn't on a paid-ads plan, the page invites them to book a call instead.
- **Social Media** — live social channels and a content calendar (powered by Vista Social),
  plus a drag-and-drop **content board** (Idea → Drafting → Scheduled → Published) and
  AI image/video tools.
- **Task Management** — a drag-and-drop board (To-Do / In Progress / Review) for the team's
  work, with assignees and due dates. Fully live and saved.
- **SEO/GEO** — a snapshot of search performance, with a button to open the full
  **Content Agent** app for the deep work.
- **Subscription** — token usage, plans, and one-time top-ups (billing connects to Stripe).
- **CRM** — your connected tools (GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Jobber) and a combined lead pipeline.

## What's live now vs. coming soon
- **Live & saving data:** logging in, switching/adding clients, the Task board, the Social
  content board, and live social channels/calendar.
- **Coming soon (shows a friendly note when clicked):** Stripe billing, the CRM tool
  connections, and the ad-platform hookups. The screens are ready — we're wiring the
  outside services next.
