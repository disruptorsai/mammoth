# Mission Control — Progress Update

Your all-in-one command center for content, ads, SEO, tasks, and clients — one login,
one place to run everything.

_Last updated: June 9, 2026_

---

## ✅ Ready to use now

- **Secure login** — your team signs in with email and password. The first account is the admin.
- **Add your clients in the app** — admins create a client in seconds (name + which tools they use).
  No setup help needed.
- **Switch between clients** — pick a client from the sidebar; everything on screen updates to them.
  Each client only sees their own workspace.
- **Task board** — a drag-and-drop board (To-Do → In Progress → Review) with owners and due dates.
  Saved automatically.
- **Content board & calendar** — plan posts from idea to published, and see the live content calendar.
- **Live social channels** — connected social accounts show real-time status.
- **Search, notifications & settings** — jump to any page or client, see recent activity, manage your account.

## 🔧 Coming soon

These screens are built and ready — we're connecting the outside services behind them:

## 📌 How to get started

1. Sign in (your account is the admin).
2. Add your first client.
3. Start adding tasks and content for that client.
